Tanmay Teckchandani


I'm a passionate freelance Nature Photographer and I love to travel, explore and capture new places through my Third Eye a.k.a Camera lens.

I enjoy traveling to new and exotic locations, especially to new Natural areas. I would rather visit a jagged peak way off the map than struggle with crowds in a city any day. For me, photography is really just a by-product of spending so much time in Nature. I think that spending time in the wilderness is just about the best thing one can do. I find a deep spirituality in nature and I hope for it to become evident in my photography. I put a lot of effort into these images and I hope that my passion for traveling and nature can translate into something special for the viewer. Enjoy looking at my images!
